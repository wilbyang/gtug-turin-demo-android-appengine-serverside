Android + App Engine

Demo application developed during Turin GTUG Bootcamp

Turin - June 5, 2012

About

This is the result of a demo app developed during the Turin GTUG Bootcamp

With an Android app you upload a photo with a caption on an app deployed on app engine (java).

The server app saves the photo on blobstore, creates and saves a thumb on the datastore and sends an asynchronous notification to the phone
